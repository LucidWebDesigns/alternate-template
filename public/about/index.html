About Us

Lucid Web Designs

Big Business Solutions for Small Enterprises

I'm Toby V, the owner and sole developer of Lucid Web Designs. My journey into programming began after I suffered heatstroke at my physical labor job. Realizing I needed a career that kept me indoors, I decided to teach myself how to code. Over three years, I taught myself to code websites by hand, reaching out to small businesses to offer my help.

What I found was eye-opening: many small businesses couldn't afford the high upfront costs of a new website. Those who did often ended up with poorly designed, outdated sites. I saw a need and decided to fill it. That's how I came up with my $0 down, $200 a month model. It's a solution that small businesses can afford, providing them with high-quality websites without breaking the bank.
